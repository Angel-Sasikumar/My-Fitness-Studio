About us

The 1st Angel's Fitness centre was opened in Anna Nagar, Chennai in 2007. in less than 10 years, with 30 centers, Angel's Fitness is the largest women only fitness chain in india.

Our Goal

To give women the confidence they need to succeed in what they do. To experience our members achieve their goals and listen to their success stories inspire us to continue offering World-class fitness programs to women across India.
Angel's Fitness has helped thousands of women in India to get healthier and stronger with its fitness solutions.
At the core of the Angel's Fitness concept is a 30-minute circuit training catered to especially to the needs of the women. This concept is complemented with personal attention to every member. In just 30 minutes, the women work every major muscle group through a proven program of strength training, cardio and stretching
The Angel's circuit coach, always keep the work out fresh with variety of exercises. The Angel's coach will motivate and offer support & advice to keep the person on the right track. At Angel's the Fit body is within reach.
