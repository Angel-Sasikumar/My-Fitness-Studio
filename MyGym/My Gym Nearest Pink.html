History of Angel's Fitness Center

We opened our first Angel's Fitness Center in Anna Nagar, Chennai in 2007. Within 14 years, we were able to open more than 45 centers across the country and we remain the largest women-only fitness center in India to date.
Over the years we have helped over 1000 women to meet their fitness goals and get stronger with training in a safe environment. The brand has over 150 combined corporate and residential projects spanning the country and has seen immense growth. Our aim is to see a country evolve from living a sedentary lifestyle to a healthy and enriched life with exercise and a balanced diet as part of its everyday routine.
